About

A sleek, sophisticated restaurant and private event space, B&P sits 500 feet above the city on the 37th floor of Two Liberty Place and features wall-to-wall unobstructed, breathtaking panoramic views that span for over 40 miles.

With its bird’s-eye views of Philadelphia and creative French cuisine by Chef Liliane , B&P sets the scene for some of the city’s most spectacular culinary experiences.
